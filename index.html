Journey!

The journey through the Computer Science program has been a transformative experience that has not only solidified my technical skills but also helped me shape my professional identity. Throughout this program, I have developed a comprehensive understanding of various areas of computer science, including software development, data structures, algorithms, artificial intelligence, and more. Each course has presented unique challenges that allowed me to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world problems, advancing both my technical and problem-solving abilities.
The capstone project was a defining element in my journey. It allowed me to consolidate the skills and knowledge acquired throughout the program into inhancing my skills in software design, algorithms and data structures, and database. Working on a full-stack web application for Travlr Getaways, I gained hands-on experience with developing and deploying applications that meet both technical and business requirements. This project was not just about coding, but also about understanding user needs, incorporating feedback, and ensuring scalability and security. It required critical thinking, adaptability, and collaboration—skills that are essential for success in the technology industry.
One of the most impactful aspects of this program has been the emphasis on understanding and integrating emerging trends and responsible computing practices. I explored topics such as artificial intelligence, data privacy, and engaged in discussions on ethical considerations in technology. These experiences have shaped my understanding of the broader impact of technology on society and the responsibility that comes with creating software solutions.
As I move forward in my career, I am confident in my ability to tackle complex technical challenges and contribute meaningfully to a collaborative environment. The Computer Science program has equipped me with a solid foundation, not only in technical knowledge but also in the principles of inclusivity, adaptability, and continuous learning. I am excited to leverage these skills in pursuing a career in software engineering and continue pushing the boundaries of what technology can achieve.
